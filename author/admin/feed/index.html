A YOUTUBE channel for DOME Marketplace – introducing our newest feature.

We are thrilled to announce the launch of our brand-new YouTube channel dedicated to  helping you dive deeper into DOME marketplace. Our mission? To demystify every aspect of DOME marketplace  through engaging and informative videos.

Creating a federated marketplace for cloud and edge services is definitely not the most trivial task there is. Even more difficult than the design itself is explaining the concept to current and future stakeholders of the platform. 

This is exactly what we strive towards, with the DOME marketplace  channel, which  is designed to provide you with comprehensive resources to master every aspect of being part of a federated marketplace for cloud and edge services.

Whether you’re a seasoned enthusiast or a curious newcomer, our channel is and will further evolve into  your go-to destination for clear, concise explanations. From introductory overviews to in-depth tutorials, we aim to have you covered. Expect to find content that breaks down complex concepts into easily digestible bites, ensuring that everyone can grasp the essence of DOME marketplace.
Plus, we’ll keep you up-to-date with the latest developments and exciting updates.

Our brand new DOMEmarketplace  newsletter has launched

Finally, our very own brand new DOMEmarketplace news channel  is out!

From now on, at the beginning of every month the DOME marketplace digest will offer you a brief overview on the most relevant news around the DOME marketplace. All information it contains is carefully pre-selected by the team, focusing on the highlights of the month. Short summaries allow you to quickly get an idea on the topic while an additional link will offer you easy access to further information if needed.

This monthly digest will not offer you updates on our most recent achievements and activities but will also provide you with interesting information from within the DOME marketplace as well the overall environment it is located in. We will share new materials and educational videos as well as upcoming events, important to the project as well as occasions for you to meet us in person.

The first edition was published at the beginning of January 2024 offering a short  review of our main milestones in  2023.  The second edition, going online at the beginning of February 2024 offers information about upcoming events, demonstrations and regulatory commitments.
